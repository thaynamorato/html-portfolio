Top Movies According to Thay

My top 3 movies of all-time

Shutter Island

This movie is a thriller that keeps you hooked from beggining to end. It's simply amazing how the plot makes you question every thing, not to talk about Lenardo Dicaprio performance which also makes you question the Oscar since he didn't get anything for it. It's a 5 star movie that I could watch a hundred times and I would notice something different every time, very recommended

The Proposal

Much different from the previous movie, this one is a romantic comedy with the amazing Sandra Bullock and the incredible Ryan Reynolds. Like most of the romantic comedies, this movie is a fun watch, it's like a comfort food but in form of a movie, that movie that can keep you happy after a stressing day of work.

The Book of Eli

I don't even have words to describe this movie, if I could pick my favorite movie of all-time I guess it would be this one. With a post-apocalyptic setting, with one of the most amazing actors, Denzel Washington, it's an action movie but so spiritual, you think you're just watching a movie when you should actually be taking a lesson from it. I can't say no more otherwise I will mess the whole plot for you, but just believe me, WATCH IT!
